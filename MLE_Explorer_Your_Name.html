Binomial Distribution — PDF / PMF

Step 1 of 5

The Binomial distribution models the number of successes in n independent Bernoulli trials, each with success probability p. The parameter to be estimated is p, with n assumed known.

Parameter: p ∈ (0,1)
Support: x = 0,1,…,n
n is fixed & known
Probability Mass Functionf(x∣p)=(nx)px(1−p)n−x,x=0,1,…,n
📌
The PMF has two parts: a combinatorial coefficient (constant w.r.t. p) and the probability piece px(1−p)n−x which carries all information about p.
